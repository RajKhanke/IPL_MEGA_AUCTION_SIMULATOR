📋 IPL 2026 MEGA AUCTION RULES
Tata IPL 2026 - Mega Auction Simulator
💰 Team Budgets
Each team has a fixed auction budget left after retention among the total budget of 120 crores. Bids must be placed within available funds. Budget decreases with each purchase. No team can exceed its total budget limit.
📈 Bid Increments
Base Price < ₹200L: Bids increase by ₹10 lakh
₹200L - ₹500L: Bids increase by ₹25 lakh
₹500L+: Bids increase by ₹50 lakh
Standard increments ensure realistic pricing.
🎯 Right To Match (RTM)
Teams can use RTM cards to retain players from their previous squad. RTM can be exercised after the highest bid is placed. If RTM is used, the retaining team matches the highest bid or passes.
👥 Squad Size & Overseas Limit
Minimum of 18 and Maximum 25 players per team is needed to complete the squad. Maximum 8 and Minimum 6 overseas players per team are needed. In each squad, at least 2 wicket keepers, 5 bowlers and 5 batsmen are required.
❌ Unsold Players
Players that receive no bids go to the Additional Set. They get a second chance to be auctioned at the end. If still unsold, they remain unsold for the season
🎪 Auction Sequence
Players are presented in sets (M1, M2 marquee... followed by Additional Set of batsman, wicket keepers, Bowlers and All rouders). Each set is auctioned in random order. Auction continues until all players are sold, unsold status is finalized, or pass.
⏱️ Bidding Process
Each player auction starts at base price. Your team can bid, pass, or AI teams can bid. Last team to bid wins the player at their bid price or base price if no bids.
